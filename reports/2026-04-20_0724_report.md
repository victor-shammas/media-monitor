## The Milan Rally: Far-Right Regrouping and Its Fractures

The **Patriots for Europe** rally at Milan's Piazza del Duomo -- hosted by **Lega**'s **Matteo Salvini** and attended by **Jordan Bardella** (**Rassemblement National**) and **Geert Wilders** (**PVV**) -- was explicitly framed as a regrouping exercise after Orbán's defeat [[28]](https://www.rfi.fr/en/international/20260419-europe-s-far-right-seeks-to-regroup-at-anti-immigration-rally-in-milan) [[29]](https://www.lefigaro.fr/politique/notre-victoire-est-proche-a-milan-jordan-bardella-fait-meeting-commun-avec-ses-allies-europeens-20260419). Yet the rally revealed internal tensions. Bardella, attending to "find allies to change the direction of European construction," reportedly expressed private irritation that the event devolved into a platform for anti-Islamic diatribes and culture-war provocations [[29]](https://www.lefigaro.fr/politique/notre-victoire-est-proche-a-milan-jordan-bardella-fait-meeting-commun-avec-ses-allies-europeens-20260419). The rally's timing -- less than a week after Orbán's fall -- made the absence of the bloc's most iconic figure impossible to ignore. The Patriots for Europe are now seeking a new symbolic centre of gravity at a moment when their ideological model's most successful practitioner has just been voted out.

The RN president lunched with the **Medef** executive committee [[33]](https://www.cnews.fr/france/2026-04-20/dejeuner-entre-jordan-bardella-et-le-medef-un-grande-premiere-pour-le-president) [[34]](https://www.liberation.fr/politique/jordan-bardella-obtient-son-rond-de-serviette-au-medef-20260420_4GKWJZEEMVCOLEU2B6SWEMQJN4/) [[35]](https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/politique/article/pourquoi-le-rendez-vous-de-jordan-bardella-avec-le-medef-crispe-autant_262835.html), with Le Pen and Bardella simultaneously dispatching a letter to business leaders pledging a "grand simplification ordinance" to "free the French economy from costly obstacles" [[36]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/info-bfmtv-la-lettre-envoyee-par-marine-le-pen-et-jordan-bardella-aux-chefs-d-entreprise-dans-le-cadre-de-la-preparation-des-echeances-electorales-majeures-de-2027_AN-202604200039.html). The Le Pen-Bardella relationship adds another layer of complexity.
