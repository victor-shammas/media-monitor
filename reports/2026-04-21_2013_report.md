## Vox: "National Priority" Becomes the Fault Line

**Vox** has secured its most significant governing foothold to date, extracting two ministries, a vice-presidency, and a regional senator's seat from **PP**'s **María Guardiola** in Extremadura -- at the cost of a 74-point agreement that includes hardline immigration measures [[140]](https://amp.rtve.es/noticias/20260421/maria-guardiola-vuelve-presentarse-investidura-junta-extremadura-apoyo-amarrado-vox/17032964.shtml). The deal's centrepiece, a "national priority" principle applying to all public subsidies, social assistance, and social housing, is now the most contentious issue in Spanish right-wing politics.

The friction within the PP is substantial. **Isabel Díaz Ayuso** and Andalusian president **Juanma Moreno** publicly questioned the measure's legal viability, with Moreno adding that Vox "does not have teams prepared to govern" [[142]](https://www.rtve.es/noticias/20260421/diferencias-pp-vox-termino-prioridad-nacional-acuerdo-extremadura/17033766.shtml). The PP's national leadership tried to reframe the clause around "arraigo" (rootedness), rather than nationality per se, in an effort to insulate it from anti-discrimination law [[142]](https://www.rtve.es/noticias/20260421/diferencias-pp-vox-termino-prioridad-nacional-acuerdo-extremadura/17033766.shtml). Vox rejected this softening: secretary general **Ignacio Garriga** explicitly pledged to force the PP to apply "national priority" without legal caps if they reach national government together, calling it a condition for any future Moncloa agreement [[147]](https://www.vozpopuli.com/espana/politica/no-vamos-a-renunciar-a-la-prioridad-nacional-y-vamos-a-forzar-al-pp-para-que-asi-sea.html) [[150]](https://www.elmundo.es/espana/2026/04/21/69e65e2c21efa0685f8b45ab.html). Vox is simultaneously leveraging the Extremadura deal as a template for negotiations underway in Aragón and Castilla y León, and as a campaign platform for upcoming Andalusian elections on 17 May [[148]](https://elpais.com/espana/elecciones-andalucia/2026-04-21/vox-promete-llevar-a-andalucia-la-prioridad-nacional-acordada-con-el-pp-en-extremadura.html).

Separately, Spain's Congress speaker moved to toughen parliamentary rules after a Vox deputy confronted parliamentary staff, with potential one-month suspensions now on the table [[145]](https://amp.rtve.es/noticias/20260421/armengol-endurece-reglamento-del-congreso-advierte-diputado-vox-se-expulsara-mes-si-reincide/17033734.shtml).
