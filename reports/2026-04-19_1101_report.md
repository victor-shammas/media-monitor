## Executive Summary

The past 24 hours revealed a mixed picture for right-wing and far-right movements globally, characterized by significant transnational mobilization alongside notable setbacks and internal challenges. European far-right leaders convened in Milan to champion "remigration" and anti-EU sentiment, directly clashing with a progressive summit in Barcelona rallying against the rise of the far-right. A major development was the definitive electoral defeat of **Viktor Orbán**'s **Fidesz** in Hungary, a key figure for European nationalists, while in the US, **Donald Trump** continued to rally supporters amidst growing scrutiny of **JD Vance**'s political standing.

### France: Rassemblement National's Strategic Positioning
**Rassemblement National** (RN) leader **Jordan Bardella** projected confidence, asserting that "our victory is close" and that an RN victory in France would be a "victory for all the nations of Europe" [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[14]](https://www.franceinfo.fr/politique/jordan-bardella/reportage-en-italie-jordan-bardella-estime-qu-une-victoire-du-rn-en-2027-serait-une-victoire-pour-toutes-les-nations-d-europe_7947992.html) [[16]](https://www.cnews.fr/videos/france/2026-04-18/notre-victoire-la-prochaine-presidentielle-est-proche-affirme-jordan). The party appears to be pursuing a dual strategy of grassroots expansion and elite engagement. The election of **Louis Aliot**, an RN mayor, as president of a major urban community signals successful local coalition-building with the mainstream right [[18]](https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/politique/article/louis-aliot-profite-d-un-deal-avec-la-droite-pour-offrir-au-rassemblement-national-un-succes-majeur_262806.html). Simultaneously, **Bardella** is actively engaging with big business leaders, seeking input on "norms and taxes that weigh on wealth production" [[17]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/jordan-bardella-va-ecrire-aux-chefs-d-entreprise-pour-solliciter-des-entretiens-et-lister-les-normes-et-impots-qui-pesent-sur-la-production-de-richesse_AN-202604180259.html) [[25]](https://www.humanite.fr/politique/entreprise/extreme-droite-le-grand-patronat-adoube-le-rn-pour-2027), a move seen as solidifying support from "the elite entrepreneurial" [[17]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/jordan-bardella-va-ecrire-aux-chefs-d-entreprise-pour-solliciter-des-entretiens-et-lister-les-normes-et-impots-qui-pesent-sur-la-production-de-richesse_AN-202604180259.html). Internally, however, tensions are emerging between **Bardella** and **Marine Le Pen**, particularly regarding strategic messaging on the European Union, with **Bardella** reportedly advocating for moderating anti-EU positions to appeal to a broader classic right-wing electorate [[22]](https://www.gala.fr/l_actu/news_de_stars/du-rififi-entre-jordan-bardella-et-marine-le-pen-a-un-an-de-la-presidentielle-la-tension-monte-il-y-a-deux-camps-20260417). His publicized relationship with a European princess has also drawn attention, potentially seen as a tool for image-softening [[19]](https://www.msn.com/fr-fr/divertissement/celebrites/je-suis-tr%C3%A8s-heureux-jordan-bardella-officialise-sa-relation-avec-maria-carolina-de-bourbon-des-deux-siciles/ar-AA20Ybvz?apiversion=v2&domshim=1&noservercache=1&noservertelemetry=1&batchservertelemetry=1&renderwebcomponents=1&wcseo=1) [[23]](https://www.gala.fr/l_actu/news_de_stars/jordan-bardella-en-couple-avec-maria-carolina-de-bourbon-des-deux-siciles-deviendra-t-il-prince-s-ils-se-marient-20260416).

## Ideological Currents and Political Strategy

The concept of "remigration" has emerged as a central, albeit controversially defined, policy plank for European far-right parties, suggesting a move beyond simply controlling borders to actively reversing demographic changes [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[27]](https://www.radiopopolare.it/il-flop-della-piazza-dei-patrioti-salvini-sempre-piu-solo-e-stanco-non-riesce-a-rilanciarsi) [[28]](https://tg24.sky.it/politica/2026/04/18/manifestazione-lega-milano-salvini). This is coupled with a persistent anti-EU sentiment, portraying Brussels as an oppressive bureaucracy [[28]](https://tg24.sky.it/politica/2026/04/18/manifestazione-lega-milano-salvini) [[30]](https://www.ansa.it/sito/notizie/politica/2026/04/18/a-milano-corteo-dei-patrioti-e-della-lega-in-piazza-duomo.-salvini-chiude_c310da29-ad7e-4873-af8f-09317aa97b3f.html). In the economic sphere, figures like former UK Prime Minister **Liz Truss** continue to advocate for radical free-market policies, criticizing centralized control and monetary policy, and explicitly backing **Bitcoin** as a counter-measure. Her move to launch **CPAC UK** signals an attempt to solidify a "sovereignty and liberty" movement [[80]](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/04/18/former-uk-prime-minister-sees-economy-on-very-negative-trajectory-indicates-support-for-bitcoin). In Sweden, the **Sverigedemokraterna** are being criticized by political opponents for betraying their working-class roots and becoming a "normal right-wing party" that prioritizes tax cuts for the wealthy and welfare cuts [[11]](https://www.dalademokraten.se/2026-04-19/lars-isacsson-s-socialdemokraterna-har-gjort-laxan/), and for allegedly operating a "troll factory" to spread "Putin's Russia"-style political culture [[10]](https://www.expressen.se/kultur/victor-malm/jimmie-akesson-har-redan-vunnit-kriget/). Culturally, concerns are being raised about the "manosphere" and its promotion of "toxic masculinity" and steroid use among young men, with serious health consequences [[50]](https://www.thetimes.com/uk/healthcare/article/steroids-silent-killer-liver-cancer-306j3mn3m) [[60]](https://johnmenadue.com/post/2026/04/from-feminism-to-the-manosphere-where-to-now/).
