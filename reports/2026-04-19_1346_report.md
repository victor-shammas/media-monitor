## Daily Intelligence Brief: Far-Right and Right-Wing Developments

**Executive Summary:** The global right-wing and far-right landscape saw a significant transnational gathering in Milan, where leaders rallied against immigration and EU policies, albeit under the shadow of **Viktor Orbán**'s recent electoral defeat in Hungary. Domestically, parties like the UK's **Reform UK** faced scrutiny over candidate conduct and financial transparency, while the **Rassemblement National** in France continued its "dediabolization" efforts through elite engagement. In the US, **Donald Trump** and **J.D. Vance** engaged with youth conservative networks, even as questions about Trump's foreign policy and the "manosphere" gained attention.

## Transnational Far-Right Mobilization and Ideology

A major convergence of European far-right leaders took place in Milan, hosted by **Matteo Salvini**'s Italian **Lega**, and featuring **Jordan Bardella** of France's **Rassemblement National** (RN) and **Geert Wilders** of the Dutch **PVV** [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[15]](https://www.boursorama.com/videos/actualites-amp/jordan-bardella-participe-a-un-meeting-de-l-extreme-droite-europeenne-a-milan-91d5a04d68dd2c096b0d31c49b911ad4) [[55]](https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20260418-europe-s-far-right-leaders-gather-in-milan-rally-against-immigration). This rally, branded "Without Fear: in Europe, Masters in our Own Home," emphasized common stances against EU bureaucracy and irregular immigration, particularly promoting "remigration" [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[28]](https://tg24.sky.it/politica/2026/04/18/manifestazione-lega-milano-salvini) [[55]](https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20260418-europe-s-far-right-leaders-gather-in-milan-rally-against-immigration). The event, however, was overshadowed by the decisive electoral defeat of Hungarian nationalist leader **Viktor Orbán**, a co-founder of the "Patriots for Europe" bloc, whose absence from Milan was notable [[14]](https://www.franceinfo.fr/politique/jordan-bardella/reportage-en-italie-jordan-bardella-estime-qu-une-victoire-du-rn-en-2027-serait-une-victoire-pour-toutes-les-nations-d-europe_7947992.html) [[30]](https://www.ansa.it/sito/notizie/politica/2026/04/18/a-milano-corteo-dei-patrioti-e-della-lega-in-piazza-duomo.-salvini-chiude_c310da29-ad7e-4873-af8f-09317aa97b3f.html) [[55]](https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20260418-europe-s-far-right-leaders-gather-in-milan-rally-against-immigration) [[59]](https://www.nin.rs/english/news/110179/collapse-of-orbans-illiberal-democracy-how-tisa-overthrew-fidesz-in-the-elections). Orbán's loss, after 16 years in power, was hailed by progressives as proof that populist governments can be defeated [[49]](https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/highlight/2026/04/19/leftist-leaders-gather-in-spain-to-rally-against-rise-of-far-right) [[59]](https://www.nin.rs/english/news/110179/collapse-of-orbans-illiberal-democracy-how-tisa-overthrew-fidesz-in-the-elections). Despite this setback, leaders like Bardella projected confidence in an impending victory for **RN** in France, stating, "Our victory is close" [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[14]](https://www.franceinfo.fr/politique/jordan-bardella/reportage-en-italie-jordan-bardella-estime-qu-une-victoire-du-rn-en-2027-serait-une-victoire-pour-toutes-les-nations-d-europe_7947992.html). Salvini, while acknowledging Orbán's defeat, supported the Hungarian leader from the Milan stage [[28]](https://tg24.sky.it/politica/2026/04/18/manifestazione-lega-milano-salvini).

In the United States, **Donald Trump** continued to engage with youth conservative networks, headlining a **Turning Point USA** "Build the Red Wall" rally in Phoenix [[75]](https://www.djournal.com/news/nation-world/photos-show-the-build-the-red-wall-rally-in-arizona-headlined-by-trump/article_69bbaea4-c1fc-5b23-b18e-c783db48fc36.html) [[78]](https://nevalleynews.org/20779/news/protesters-line-the-streets-as-maga-supporters-trickle-in-to-the-turning-point-usa-event-with-trump-in-phoenix/) [[81]](https://www.kjzz.org/photos-president-donald-trump-attends-phoenix-turning-point-usa-rally). ## Immigration, Borders, and National Identity

The Milan rally served as a platform for intensified rhetoric on immigration, with leaders advocating for "remigration" policies, interpreted as mass expulsions or strict repatriations of irregular migrants [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[27]](https://www.radiopopolare.it/il-flop-della-piazza-dei-patrioti-salvini-sempre-piu-solo-e-stanco-non-riesce-a-rilanciarsi) [[28]](https://tg24.sky.it/politica/2026/04/18/manifestazione-lega-milano-salvini). **Geert Wilders** warned of a "tsunami of mass immigration, illegal immigration, mostly from Islamic countries," emphasizing a perceived threat to "the original inhabitants of Europe" [[12]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/notre-victoire-est-proche-jordan-bardella-rassure-ses-allies-europeens-au-rassemblement-de-l-extreme-droite-a-milan_AD-202604180345.html) [[55]](https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20260418-europe-s-far-right-leaders-gather-in-milan-rally-against-immigration). **Matteo Salvini** attempted to soften the term "remigration" to mean conditional residency based on good behavior, akin to a "driving license with points," yet also called for criminals to be sent "home" [[28]](https://tg24.sky.it/politica/2026/04/18/manifestazione-lega-milano-salvini) [[29]](https://www.rainews.it/articoli/2026/04/corteo-lega-a-milano-cori-contro-musulmani-e-von-der-leyen-155841a6-f3f8-4cc1-8305-b7f306c96d52.html). This framing echoes broader far-right narratives seen in the UK, where **Britain First** organized a St. George's Day march to protest the "extinction" of Britain due to "invaders" [[57]](https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/home-news/britain-first-march-manchester-arrest-police-b2960357.html).

In the UK, **Reform UK**'s vetting procedures for local election candidates were criticized as "not fit for purpose" after further accusations of racist and anti-Islam social media posts emerged, including comments calling Islam "the religion of rape, incest and paedophilia" [[39]](https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2026/apr/18/two-more-reform-local-election-candidates-accused-offensive-posts). **Nigel Farage** also proposed that outer London boroughs like Bromley and Havering should vote to leave London and rejoin their historical counties, implicitly linking this to preserving areas with demographics comparable to the broader UK [[41]](https://unherd.com/newsroom/nigel-farage-shouldnt-let-bromley-leave-london/) [[42]](https://unherd.com/newsroom/nigel-farage-shouldnt-let-bromley-leave-london/?edition=us). The debate around immigration was further fueled by an article from the BBC, which was accused by experts of "hyping" immigration issues and platforming right-wing figures, thereby "fuelling a false concept of far-right populism" [[44]](https://www.thenational.scot/news/26030193.bbc-hype-around-immigration-playing-far-right-hands/). Support for anti-immigration activism was also visible with celebrity **Sharon Osbourne** publicly backing a rally organized by far-right activist **Tommy Robinson**, leading a homelessness charity to cut ties with her due to the event's misalignment with their values [[56]](https://www.unilad.com/celebrity/news/sharon-osbourne-charity-cut-ties-far-right-activist-291736-20260418) [[69]](https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/home-news/sharon-osbourne-tommy-robinson-rally-centrepoint-b2960135.html) [[71]](https://www.nme.com/news/music/homelessness-charity-cuts-ties-with-sharon-osbourne-after-she-appears-to-lend-support-to-tommy-robinson-far-right-rally-this-sort-of-event-does-not-align-with-our-values-3941007).

## Right-Wing Political Challenges and Internal Dynamics

Several right-wing parties are navigating internal and external pressures. In France, **Jordan Bardella**'s **Rassemblement National** is actively pursuing a strategy of "dediabolization," marked by engagement with the business elite, including a luncheon with the Medef executive bureau and a dinner with CAC 40 leaders [[13]](https://www.lejdd.fr/politique/rn-marine-le-pen-a-la-table-des-grands-patrons-171825) [[17]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/jordan-bardella-va-ecrire-aux-chefs-d-entreprise-pour-solliciter-des-entretiens-et-lister-les-normes-et-impots-qui-pesent-sur-la-production-de-richesse_AN-202604180259.html) [[25]](https://www.humanite.fr/politique/entreprise/extreme-droite-le-grand-patronat-adoube-le-rn-pour-2027). Bardella explicitly stated that winning in 2027 requires both the "popular bloc and the entrepreneurial elite" [[17]](https://www.bfmtv.com/politique/front-national/jordan-bardella-va-ecrire-aux-chefs-d-entreprise-pour-solliciter-des-entretiens-et-lister-les-normes-et-impots-qui-pesent-sur-la-production-de-richesse_AN-202604180259.html). This push, however, has reportedly created internal tensions with **Marine Le Pen**, particularly over moderating stances on the European Union, which some within the party view as a departure from established party lines [[22]](https://www.gala.fr/l_actu/news_de_stars/du-rififi-entre-jordan-bardella-et-marine-le-pen-a-un-an-de-la-presidentielle-la-tension-monte-il-y-a-deux-camps-20260417). The party also secured a significant local success with **Louis Aliot**'s election as president of the Perpignan urban community, achieved through a deal with local right-wing parties, further demonstrating a strategy of broad appeal and alliance building [[18]](https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/politique/article/louis-aliot-profite-d-un-deal-avec-la-droite-pour-offrir-au-rassemblement-national-un-succes-majeur_262806.html).

In Sweden, the **Sverigedemokraterna** (SD) were accused of operating a "troll factory" to produce digital propaganda, engaging in "gross racism and coordinated hate attacks," and showing "a nonchalant attitude to truth," with critics comparing their political culture to "Putin's Russia" [[10]](https://www.expressen.se/kultur/victor-malm/jimmie-akesson-har-redan-vunnit-kriget/). This portrayal contrasts with their characterization by some as having become a "usual right-wing party" that prioritizes tax cuts for the wealthy over social welfare [[11]](https://www.dalademokraten.se/2026-04-19/lars-isacsson-s-socialdemokraterna-har-gjort-laxan/). In Norway, the **Fremskrittspartiet** is engaged in budget negotiations with **Høyre** [[8]](https://www.sandnesposten.no/onsker-ikke-at-kine-overgangen-skal-komme-mellom-frp-og-hoyre/s/5-105-1349202) and faced an awkward incident involving a miscommunicated joint invitation to a union seminar with the far-left **Rødt** [[9]](https://www.aftenposten.no/norge/politikk/i/aJO922/marie-sneve-martinussen-sendte-ut-fellesinvitasjon-fra-roedt-og-frp-da-reagerte-frp-toppen). Meanwhile, Italy's **Matteo Salvini** appears to be facing a decline in influence, with a "semivoid square" at his Milan rally suggesting diminishing public appeal and growing isolation [[27]](https://www.radiopopolare.it/il-flop-della-piazza-dei-patrioti-salvini-sempre-piu-solo-e-stanco-non-riesce-a-rilanciarsi). His **Lega** party is also contending with competition from emerging ultra-right factions [[31]](https://ilmanifesto.it/salvini-sfida-il-25-aprile-milano-prepara-la-risposta) [[58]](https://www.straitstimes.com/world/europe/europes-far-right-rails-at-migration-tsunami-after-defeat-of-hungarys-orban), while the center-right **Forza Italia** has subtly challenged Salvini's anti-immigrant rhetoric by holding its own event advocating for migrant integration [[33]](https://www.radiopopolare.it/il-dito-nellocchio-di-forza-italia-a-salvini-nel-giorno-della-remigrazione-si-allintegrazione-dei-migranti). In the UK, **Reform UK** deputy leader **Richard Tice** faced scrutiny over his companies' alleged failure to pay nearly £100,000 in corporation tax, with total avoided/unpaid tax linked to his entities potentially reaching almost £800,000, raising questions about financial probity within the party [[34]](https://nation.cymru/news/tice-says-errors-bound-to-happen-after-reports-he-failed-to-pay-100000-tax/) [[36]](https://www.thetimes.com/uk/politics/article/richard-tice-corporation-tax-reform-ttq9cp60f). Separately, **Nigel Farage**'s personal income, including £675,000 from foreign sources since becoming an MP, also drew criticism [[37]](https://www.desmog.com/2026/04/18/mapped-nigel-farage-reform-uk-money-income-donations-tracker/) [[38]](https://www.desmog.com/2026/04/18/nigel-farage-income-foreign-donors-governments-companies/).

Discussions around social conservatism and identity continue to animate right-wing discourse. In the US, the intersection of religion and politics within the **MAGA** movement was highlighted, with an article observing the enduring influence of organized religion in politics and the Trump administration's perceived amorality in matters of war [[4]](https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2026/apr/18/trump-pope-war-morals). Broader societal trends intersecting with conservative concerns include the "manosphere" phenomenon, which was linked to rising steroid use, associated health risks, and the promotion of misogynistic views and a specific ideal of masculinity [[50]](https://www.thetimes.com/uk/healthcare/article/steroids-silent-killer-liver-cancer-306j3mn3m). This online subculture is seen as contributing to fractured gender relations, declining birth rates, and increased loneliness, prompting reflections on the disruptions caused by evolving gender dynamics and the conservative "red pill brigade" [[50]](https://www.thetimes.com/uk/healthcare/article/steroids-silent-killer-liver-cancer-306j3mn3m) [[60]](https://johnmenadue.com/post/2026/04/from-feminism-to-the-manosphere-where-to-now/).
