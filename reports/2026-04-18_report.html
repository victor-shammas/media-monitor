European Far-Right Unity and Challenges

This transnational gathering aimed to project unity and revitalize the Lega [98]. However, this show of force occurred amidst significant challenges for the broader European populist right, particularly following Viktor Orbán's electoral setback in Hungary, which saw his Fidesz party lose symbolic strongholds [236].
