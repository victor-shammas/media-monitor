## Economic Philosophy and Fiscal Debates
Right-wing economic discourse continues to champion reduced taxation, deregulation, and critiques of centralized financial systems. Former UK Prime Minister **Liz Truss** advocated for lower taxes, diminished regulation, and questioned the role of central banking, positioning **Bitcoin** as part of a broader resistance against "centralized control" and currency "debasement" [[80]](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/04/18/former-uk-prime-minister-sees-economy-on-very-negative-trajectory-indicates-support-for-bitcoin). Her perspective reflects a segment of the right that attributes economic stagnation to excessive government intervention and inflationary monetary policies [[80]](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/04/18/former-uk-prime-minister-sees-economy-on-very-negative-trajectory-indicates-support-for-bitcoin).

Conversely, **Reform UK**'s deputy leader **Richard Tice** faced public scrutiny over reports that his companies avoided nearly £100,000 in corporation tax, a development potentially undermining the party's image as a proponent of fiscal responsibility [[34]](https://nation.cymru/news/tice-says-errors-bound-to-happen-after-reports-he-failed-to-pay-100000-tax/) [[35]](https://taxpolicy.org.uk/2026/04/18/richard-tice-quidnet-reit-corporation-tax/) [[36]](https://www.thetimes.com/uk/politics/article/richard-tice-corporation-tax-reform-ttq9cp60f). The incident highlights the inherent tension between advocating for "tax efficiency" as a corporate duty and fulfilling tax liabilities [[34]](https://nation.cymru/news/tice-says-errors-bound-to-happen-after-reports-he-failed-to-pay-100000-tax/) [[35]](https://taxpolicy.org.uk/2026/04/18/richard-tice-quidnet-reit-corporation-tax/).

In Sweden, the **Sverigedemokraterna** are criticized for implementing tax cuts that favor the affluent while concurrently reducing welfare provisions, leading to accusations of abandoning working-class constituents and aligning with traditional right-wing economic policies [[11]](https://www.dalademokraten.se/2026-04-19/lars-isacsson-s-socialdemokraterna-har-gjort-laxan/). This narrative is strategically utilized by center-left parties to expose perceived inconsistencies within populist right-wing movements.
